Introduction

SRCCL example The application makes graphics by taking data from specifically formatted Excel sheets. The detailed information on the input formats is provided in Examples. As a starting point, you may download the 'SRCCL example'. It is based on tables published in the supplementary material of IPCC's Special Report on Climate Change and Land (SRCCL).

Examples can be used to test the application as well as as a template to provide your own data. To make graphs, follow the simple procedure shown below.

Feedback is welcome, including on what you would like to have as input format, layout or other issues that you may experience, or missing features.
